**Conversation Description** - Mister Black, Checkmate and Permabull Nino discuss the state of macro and crypto markets in the context of collapsing economies and unlimited QE. The conversation considers the nature of sound money and how Gold, Bitcoin and Decred represent different areas of the sound money spectrum.


## **Events happening**
- COVID, unemployment and markets rallying

- Unlimited QE and Liqudity of Dollars

- Gold Price continues strength

## **Topics for discussion:**

**Nature of Sound Money**

    - Inflation resistance (decentralised, no once point of control)
    - Gold as historical money turned into a hedge
    - Bitcoin as the genesis of digital gold
    - Decred as a contender with a unique set of value propositions

**GOLD**

    - Central banks accumulating in the East
    - Gold suppression scheme
    - Worldwide production
    - Impact of a gold standard

**BITCOIN**

    - Digital scarcity
    - Trivial to verify, transport and store
    - Resistance to confiscation

**Decred**

    - Contrarian bet and hedge
    - Hedges
        - Pure PoW Security and Distribution
        - Rough Consensus
        - Ability to adapt
        - Evolution of features
        - Self-funded
