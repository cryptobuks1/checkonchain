## Supply Side Dynamics

Decred has a unique supply side dynamic with four primary entities who receive freshly minted DCR coins from the block subsidy:

- Proof-of-Work Miners (60%)
- Proof-of-Stake Stakeholders (30%)
- Proof-of-Skill Contractors (varies, max of 10%)
- Decred Treasury (10% minus contractor payments)

It is important to distinguish between Decred contractors, and the Decred Treasury as separate entities. Until very recently, the incoming DCR via the block reward each month has exceeded the volume of DCR being spent.
