# **Decred Charting Suite**

## **Network Valuation Metrics**

These metrics analyse the global Decred network valuation (i.e. Market Cap, Realised Cap, Global block subsidy models...)
___

[**Block Subsidy Model (USD)**]('dcr_charts\valuation_models\block_subsidy_usd_valuation.html) - Presents the cumulative block reward paid to Miners, Stakeholders, the Treasury and on aggregate. This metric was developed by [@permabullnino](https://twitter.com/PermabullNino) ([paper](https://medium.com/@permabullnino/decred-on-chain-a-look-at-block-subsidies-6f5180932c9b)) and represents the aggregate cost basis for each group of network participants.
